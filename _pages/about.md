Hi there! I'm Szu-Yu (Eva) Mo (莫絲羽), a roboticist-turned-builder from Taiwan. These days I'm CTO / Founding Engineer at [Athleo.ai](https://athleo.ai/), an AI-powered sports video storytelling platform that turns raw footage into highlights worth sharing. On the side, I'm building [TransFOMO](https://transfomo.app/), a social app that helps even the most introverted friends feel welcome enough to show up. I'm always up for a good conversation — and stay open to interesting robotics / computer vision opportunities, so please don't be shy to [reach out](mailto:evamo.tw1@gmail.com)!

Before jumping into the startup world, I spent nearly five years at [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/) as a computer vision engineer in the autonomous driving department, working on real-time mapping and 4D auto-labeling pipelines for self-driving cars. I hold a Master of Science in Robotics System Development ([MRSD](https://mrsd.ri.cmu.edu/)) from Carnegie Mellon University (2021) and a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University ([NTUEE](https://web.ee.ntu.edu.tw/), 2019).

My love for robotics and computer vision started early. During my undergrad research at NTU's [Intelligent Robot Lab](https://robotlab.csie.ntu.edu.tw/) with Prof. [Li-Chen Fu](http://www.ee.ntu.edu.tw/bio?id=23), I dove into semantic visual SLAM with ROS and Python. At CMU, I led parts of [AutoValet](https://mrsdprojects.ri.cmu.edu/2020teami/), a 1.5-year capstone with three teammates where we built an autonomous parking system for indoor garages — you can watch our final [demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bb5E7sUFKGY&list=LL&index=12) here. Along the way, I interned at [Coretronic](https://www.coretronic.com) (monocular visual SLAM), [PicCollage](https://piccollage.com/) (semantic segmentation for the app's [person cut-out](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/piccollage-fun-layout-editor/id448639966) feature), and NVIDIA.

When I'm not coding, I love telling stories. I write on Medium — both an [English account](https://medium.com/@evamo0508) and a Mandarin [publication](https://medium.com/momo%E7%9A%84%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8%E4%BA%BA%E7%95%99%E5%AD%B8%E5%A4%A2) about studying abroad, life as a software engineer, and the occasional nerdy project (Google Translate does a decent job on the Mandarin ones!). I'm also a budding video creator under the handle **momo_collection_** — on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/momo_collection_/) I share reflective vlogs about leaving NVIDIA, building a life I actually want, and easing into startup life, while my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@momo_collection_) is where I unwind with laid-back camping and travel adventures. Same name, two very different vibes! I'd genuinely love to hear from you if any of it resonates.

Nice to meet you! Together, let's build a better world.
